Lykan Hypersport

About

The Lykan Hypersport is a limited-production supercar manufactured by W Motors, a UAE-based company. Introduced in 2013, it is known for its aggressive styling, futuristic interior, and luxurious features. What makes it stand out is its diamond-encrusted LED headlights, a holographic infotainment system, and an ultra-exotic appeal. With only seven units ever built, it remains one of the rarest supercars in history.

Biography

The Lykan Hypersport is powered by a 3.7-liter twin-turbocharged flat-six engine developed by RUF, producing 780 horsepower and 960 Nm of torque. It can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h (0-62 mph) in just 2.8 seconds and reach a top speed of 395 km/h (245 mph). The car gained massive recognition when it was featured in "Furious 7," showcasing its speed and exclusivity. Its price tag of $3.4 million makes it one of the most expensive production cars in the world, appealing to elite collectors and automotive enthusiasts alike.
